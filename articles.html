Is The Concept Of Willpower a Myth?

We have been taught that we can resist anything with sheer willpower but it's not as easy as it seems.Being productive is a lot easier said than done. Often we feel swamped with work and feel 'productive' when really we were simply busy and not making the absolute best of our time. And with the notion of productivity getting more and more popular, there are plenty of tips and tricks out there to maximize output.
